Introduction

Bears, which are adorable, robust, and magnificent creatures, are mammals belonging to the Ursidae family and typically exhibit an omnivorous diet. Bears play significant ecological roles, contributing to seed dispersal, regulating prey populations, and shaping their ecosystems.
